  • Schwurbelkasten
  • Gandolf´s

    • Schwurbelkasten is a digital modular system that allows users to create their own conspiracy worlds. Originating from the German words ‘schwurbeln’ (talking about conspiracies) and ‘Kasten’ (box), Schwurbelkasten aims to highlight the absurdity and arbitrariness of conspiracy constructs and the internet’s role of providing a breeding ground for them. With various digitally illustrated components inspired by typical elements of real conspiracy narratives, individual stories can be constructed playfully. An accompanying news ticker continuously displays current conspiracy content, cited from social media.
